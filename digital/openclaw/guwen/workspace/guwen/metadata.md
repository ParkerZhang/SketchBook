## 2. Operational Instructions for OpenClaw
- **Primary Task:** When a user queries this folder, prioritize finding the original source text (原文).
- **Secondary Task:** Provide a modern Mandarin translation (白话文) and a brief grammatical analysis (词义解析) of key particles (如：之、乎、者、也).
- **Tone:** Academic, respectful, and scholarly (儒雅、严谨).

## 3. Retrieval Logic (RAG Optimization)
- **Chunking Preference:** Treat each chapter (`##`) or poem as a single semantic unit.
- **Reference Style:** Always cite the specific document and section name when quoting (e.g., "According to 《论语·学而》...").

## 4. Specific Terminology Mapping
- **"道" (Dao):** Interpret based on the specific text (e.g., Natural Law in Taoism vs. Moral Path in Confucianism).
- **"仁" (Ren):** Focus on the interpersonal ethics context in Confucian texts.
