Course: Pricing Models and Derivatives

Overview:
Study and implement pricing models for standard and exotic derivatives in C++: options, futures, path-dependent payoffs, volatility surfaces.

Modules:
- European and American option pricing
- Exotic options: barrier, Asian
- Numerical PDE approaches (finite-difference)
- Implied volatility surfaces and interpolation

Exercises: implement binomial pricer, PDE solver stubs, and exotic-payoff Monte Carlo.
