Course: Fixed Income & Derivatives

Overview:
Build pricing and analytics for bonds, swaps, and fixed-income derivatives: yield curve construction, bootstrapping, duration, convexity, interest-rate models.

Modules:
- Bond pricing and yield measures
- Curve bootstrapping and interpolation
- Interest rate swaps and valuation
- Short-rate and multi-factor models

Exercises: bootstrapping implementation, swap valuation tool, curve plotting helpers.
