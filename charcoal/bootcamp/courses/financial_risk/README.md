Course: Financial Risk Engineering

Overview:
Foundations and practical methods for measuring and managing financial risk using C++: Value-at-Risk (VaR), CVaR, stress testing, scenario modeling, risk dashboards.

Modules:
- Risk measures: VaR, CVaR
- Historical and parametric VaR
- Monte Carlo risk simulations
- Portfolio aggregation and factor models
- Stress testing and scenario analysis

Exercises: build VaR calculators, backtest routines, and report generators.
